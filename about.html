Hi, I am Yi Xin, a UI/UX designer at GovTech, the Government Technology Agency of Singapore. I design products that aims to improve the lives of citizens.

My most recent work is Beeline, a crowdsource transport experiment, and the Digital Parking App, a system that replaces the current paper parking coupon. Prior to Beeline and Digital Parking, I worked on One Upon Light, a monochromatic puzzle game about light and shadows. I enjoy working in a dynamic and collaborative environment where every team member comes from a different background and has something to contribute.

While it was fun and challenging making games, the rational side of me aspires to contribute to meaningful products that can impact people’s life more directly. I personally believe that the real world can benefit from more design! Otherwise, game development and product development share a lot in common- from tools, processes to roles and dynamics. And I am also learning Javascript, so that I can emphatise with developers even more to make great products.

Outside of designing digital stuff, I swim, cycle and obsess about Scandinavian and mid-century furniture. I enjoy taking bokehlicious snaps too. And a good cup of milk green tea with lots of froth!
